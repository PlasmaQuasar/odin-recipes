Penne alla Vodka

Penne pasta cooked with Vodka and topped

with Basil, Parmesan, and Pepper Flakes

Penne alla vodka is a pasta dish made with vodka

and penne pasta, usually made with heavy cream,

crushed tomatoes, onions, and sometimes sausage,

pancetta or peas. The recipe became very popular

in Italy and in the United States around the 1980s,

when it was offered to discotheque customers.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Penne Pasta
  • 1/4 cup Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 4 Cloves Garlic, Minced
  • 1 tsp Red Pepper Flakes
  • 28 oz Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1/2 cup Heavy Whipping Cream
  • 2 tbsp Vodka
  • Chopped Basil to Taste

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add penne and cook, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 11 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and red pepper flakes; cook and stir until garlic is golden brown.
  3. Add tomatoes and salt; bring to boil. Reduce heat and simmer for 15 minutes.
  4. Add cream and vodka; bring to boil. Reduce heat to low and add pasta; toss for 1 minute. Stir in basil; serve warm.
